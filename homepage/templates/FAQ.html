What is this?

This is a website designed to gather all the latest restaurant deals near you.

Who is this for?

Anyone who wants to save money and help local restaurants!

How do I use this?

Click on the "Food Deals" tab at the top and it will direct you to a list of restaurants and the deals that they are offering.
Go to the restaurant and mention the deal when ordering.

What's the catch?

You have to order in person! This way, restaurants are saving money from extra third-party fees, and you get to save money on food!
